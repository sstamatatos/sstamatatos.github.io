About

Goals:

The goal of the Tax Correction Group, LLC. is to ensure that homeowners receive their fair share of tax savings.

The Tax Correction Group, LLC. is a limited-liability corporation based out of Huntington, New York. Our organization was founded based on the idea that every person deserves the tax exemptions for which they are eligible, and that nobody should lose money because of the complicated filing process. As such, we strive to make filing as easy as possible. We also charge a low price to ensure that our customers can maximize the amount of money they gain from working with us.

We help homeowners file for tax exemptions, and we ensure that all tax deductions are being applied. We also help to make the filing process smooth and manageable for all homeowners. It is our goal to make sure that homeowners receive their fair share of tax savings.

Our experts handle the difficult aspects of the procedure; we understand that the tax exemption process can be arduous. That is why our customized process is designed to make the reception of tax exemptions as painless as possible.

History:

The Tax Correction Group was founded by experts

The Tax Correction Group, LLC. was founded by Sebastian Stamatatos in his junior year of high school. At that time, he discovered an interest in real estate and the housing market after performing an award-winning science research project on the correlation between over-assesment of a home’s value and its days on market (DOM). While performing this research, Sebastian discovered some of the nuances of tax records and subsequently realized that even some of his neighbors were not taking full advantage of the exemptions available to them! Thus, the Tax Correction group was formed to provide a service to the community, and to help Sebastian raise funds to further his education after high school.
